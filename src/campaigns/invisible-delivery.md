## What we're seeing

Transport investment is often announced at a national level — funding approved, projects confirmed, strategies published — before any physical work begins locally. The gap between announcement and delivery can stretch over years. During that time, communities may not know what has been decided, what is coming, or when to expect it.

This campaign tracks specific announcements and monitors whether and when they become visible on the ground.

## The gap problem

The gap between announcement and delivery is a communication and accountability issue as much as a construction one. When a bus route improvement is announced but the timetable has not changed, when a footpath is funded but not yet built, or when a cycling scheme appears in a strategy document but not on any road — the gap between those two realities is worth documenting.
